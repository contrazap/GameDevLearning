# knowledge/

Your personal wiki — one note per finished feature, closed via the **from-memory ritual** (CONVENTIONS §Knowledge notes): you answer the plan's prompts aloud without looking anything up; Claude transcribes and drafts the mechanics sections from the code; the note ends with `Had to look up:`.

The notes do three jobs here:
1. **Reference** — the only material (besides official docs) allowed under the no-copy rule when re-implementing a pattern in a later project.
2. **Mode steering** — the generator reads rep counts and `Had to look up:` lines to pick guided/how-to/story and to demote skills that didn't stick.
3. **Teach calibration** — what memory didn't hold tells future Teach blocks what to emphasize.

Naming: `KN-<short-topic>.md`, e.g. `KN-enhanced-input.md`, `KN-blender-export.md` (that one gets written at B1.4 and becomes the most-referenced note in the repo).
